# BEAM Indigo

> **Bridging Empowerment, Achieving Mastery**

BEAM Indigo is a mobile wellness and personal development platform designed to help individuals cultivate self-awareness, build healthier habits, and achieve meaningful personal growth. By bringing together guided reflection, emotional wellness tools, goal management, and habit tracking into one cohesive experience, BEAM Indigo serves as the bridge between where users are today and where they aspire to be.

Built on the philosophy of **Bridging Empowerment, Achieving Mastery**, the application empowers users to transform small, intentional actions into lasting personal change through consistency, reflection, and measurable progress.

---

## 🌟 Vision

To empower individuals to become the best version of themselves by providing a connected ecosystem of wellness tools, personal growth resources, and meaningful insights that support lifelong self-development.

---

## 🎯 Mission

BEAM Indigo bridges the gap between intention and transformation by helping users develop sustainable habits, strengthen emotional resilience, and achieve mastery through guided reflection, goal setting, and continuous personal growth.

---

## ❓ Problem Statement

Many people are committed to improving their mental, emotional, and physical well-being but struggle because the tools they need are spread across multiple applications. Journaling, mood tracking, habit building, and goal management often exist independently, making it difficult to stay consistent and recognize meaningful patterns over time.

Without a centralized platform, users frequently lose motivation, abandon healthy routines, and miss opportunities to better understand themselves through long-term reflection.

---

## 💡 Solution

BEAM Indigo provides a secure, cloud-connected platform that combines wellness tools into a single mobile experience. Users can track moods, complete guided journal entries, build positive habits, manage personal goals, receive reminders, and monitor their progress through visual analytics and achievements.

Rather than focusing on perfection, BEAM Indigo encourages continuous growth through small, consistent actions that lead to meaningful, lasting change.

---

## ✨ Core Features

* Secure user authentication with Firebase Authentication
* Personalized user profiles
* Guided Shadow Work Journal
* Daily mood tracking and emotional trend analysis
* SMART goal creation and progress tracking
* Daily habit tracker
* Achievement badges and milestone rewards
* Personalized wellness dashboard
* Push notifications and daily reminders
* Cloud synchronization with Firebase Firestore
* AI-assisted reflection prompts and wellness insights *(planned research component)*
